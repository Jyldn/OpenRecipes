Banana Bread

Especially good for bananas you'd otherwise throw away.

Ingredients

  • 3 mashed bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 2.5 dessert spoon of olive oil (or butter, or combo)
  • Cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp baking soda
  • Bit over half cup oats, blended (add extra at end if recipe is too runny)
  • Bit over half cup dessicated coconut, blended
  • Dash maple syrup
  • Half cup frozen blueberries (optional)

Directions

Preheat oven to 180 celsius. Mash the banana, then add eggs, butter/oil, maple syrup & mix. Once that's mixed, add the dry ingredients, then add the blueberries at the end. Pour batter into lined bread tin. If you want to be fancy, you can slice a banana length wise and place each half on top of the batter in the bread tin (spread a little butter and sugar on top of the banana slices if you want them to caramelise).

Bake for around 35/40 minutes, until it looks golden and yummy.
